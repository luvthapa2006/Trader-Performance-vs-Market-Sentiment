# Project Summary

## Objective

The objective of this project was to study the relationship between Bitcoin market sentiment and trader performance using Hyperliquid trading data.

---

## Methodology

- Loaded both datasets
- Checked missing values and duplicates
- Converted timestamps
- Merged datasets by date
- Calculated Daily PnL
- Computed Win Rate
- Measured Average Trade Size
- Counted Daily Trades
- Compared Buy vs Sell trades
- Segmented traders based on activity

---

## Insights

### Insight 1

Extreme Greed generated the highest average Closed PnL.

### Insight 2

Fear market conditions recorded the largest number of trades.

### Insight 3

SELL trades slightly exceeded BUY trades in most market conditions.

---

## Strategy Recommendations

### Strategy 1

Increase exposure carefully during Extreme Greed while maintaining disciplined risk management.

### Strategy 2

Reduce overtrading during Fear periods and prioritize high-conviction trade setups.

---

## Conclusion

Market sentiment influences both trading behavior and profitability. Extreme Greed showed the strongest average profitability, while Fear periods generated the highest trading activity.
